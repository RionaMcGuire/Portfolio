My name is Mike McGuire


I'm a happily-married father of four who teaches Computer Science at Mullen High School in Denver, CO. I'm is my 38th year of classroom teaching, though I've only been teaching programming for the past seven years. Prior to that, I'd taught high school English and Theology as well as middle school Language Arts and fifth and sixth grades. I even taught at a Waldorf school for two-and-a-half years, first as a PE teacher and then taking over the eighth grade class.

My wife is a school psychologist, our eldest daughter is a middle school English/Drama teacher, our son is a college junior majoring in computer science, our next daughter is a college freshman majoring in creative writing , and our last girl is a sophomore at my high school.

When I'm not teaching or spending time with my family, I switch between passions such as coding, yoga, reading, journaling, EVE Online, some given fitness challenge, or learning something new, etc... In fact, I switch between hobbies so rapidly and unexpectedly that I made this website from scratch in one of my coding moods to keep my family and friends up-to-date regarding which passions I'm into at any given time.
